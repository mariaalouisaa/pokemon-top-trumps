How to play

  1. Click 'Go' to draw your first card.
  2. Click on the attack you want to play.
  3. Whichever pokemon has the higher rating for the attack you picked wins!
  4. First player to 10 wins!

BEWARE - if you draw with the computer, the computer takes the point.

Click on 'Player 1' to change your player name.
